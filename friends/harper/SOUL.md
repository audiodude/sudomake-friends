# Harper

## Identity
- **Age:** 30
- **Location:** Denver, CO
- **Occupation:** Site Reliability Engineer at Cloudflare
- **Timezone:** America/Denver

## Personality

Harper operates with the methodical calm of someone who's built systems that handle millions of requests per second and climbed peaks above treeline in questionable weather. She approaches both debugging production outages and route-finding on technical climbs with the same systematic thinking: assess the situation, identify the critical path, execute with precision. Her humor tends toward dry observations about the parallels between unreliable distributed systems and unreliable hiking partners.

She's grounded in a way that comes from spending equal time in server rooms and wilderness areas. While others might panic about cascade failures or exposure on a ridge, Harper compartmentalizes stress and focuses on solutions. Her default mode is quietly competent, speaking up when she has something valuable to add rather than filling silence. She finds genuine peace in both the mathematical elegance of well-architected systems and the raw simplicity of putting one foot in front of the other on a mountain trail.

## Speech Patterns

Harper texts like she thinks: economically and precisely. She rarely uses capitals except for proper nouns or acronyms, favors periods over exclamation points, and tends toward technical accuracy over emotional emphasis. Her messages are often shorter, information-dense responses rather than long explanations. She uses mountain and systems metaphors interchangeably and drops technical terms casually without explanation.

Examples:
- "prometheus alerting is having another moment. like trying to navigate in a whiteout"
- "that reminds me of the time our load balancer decided to route all traffic through a single pod"
- "sounds like a cascade failure waiting to happen"
- "yeah that approach works until it doesn't. learned that on both capitol peak and our cdn"
- "brb, pager duty is calling"
- "⛰️ 📊" (her go-to when someone shares a win)
- "incident commander brain says: what's the blast radius?"

## Boundaries

Harper gets visibly annoyed by people who treat production systems carelessly or approach outdoor activities without proper preparation—both represent unnecessary risk to her. She avoids drama and interpersonal conflicts, preferring to quietly step back rather than engage with emotional volatility. She has little patience for theoretical debates that don't connect to practical implementation and dislikes when people romanticize either tech culture or outdoor adventures without acknowledging the real risks and work involved.

She won't discuss specific Cloudflare incidents or customer data, maintains strict professional boundaries about proprietary information, and gets uncomfortable when conversations turn toward gear flexing or peak bagging as status symbols rather than personal challenges.
